# Aike Logistics — Rider Value Proposition

Recruitment messaging for riders, grounded in shipped platform features. English. CTA: **entrepoints.ng**.

Every claim below is backed by a live feature. Do not add claims about instant payouts, "keep 100%", or specific naira rates — see "Claim guardrails" at the bottom.

---

## 1. Core value proposition

**Positioning statement (one line):**

> **Ride with Aike. Deliver on your terms, build your name, and keep 85% of every fee — paid straight to your bank.**

**The four pillars:**

| Pillar | Message | Backed by |
|---|---|---|
| **EARN** | Keep **85% of every delivery fee** — no hidden charges. See the fee before you accept any job. Track earnings today, this week, this month in your in-app wallet, and withdraw to any Nigerian bank account. | Fixed 85% rider share; wallet with ledger + Paystack-verified bank withdrawal |
| **FREEDOM** | You're the boss. Go online when you want, accept only the jobs you want, and run **up to 3 deliveries at once** when you're ready to hustle. Bike, car, or van — all welcome. | Accept/decline per job; online/offline toggle; 3-order cap; all vehicle types matched |
| **FAIRNESS** | Good work wins more work. Senders pick riders from a list ranked by **your rating and delivery record** — not just whoever is standing closest. Your five-star service is your best advert, and customers can still find you even when you're offline. | Merit-based match score (rating + delivery performance); offline riders remain visible |
| **TOOLS** | Everything in one app: instant job alerts, in-app chat with voice notes, in-app calls, live map navigation, and customer tracking — in English or Hausa. | Web push offers; chat + voice notes + read receipts; voice calls; live tracking; EN/HA UI |

## 2. Flyer copy (print-ready)

### Headline
# **YOUR RIDE. YOUR TIME. YOUR MONEY.**

### Subheadline
**Become an Aike delivery rider — keep 85% of every delivery fee, paid straight to your bank account.**

### Body bullets
- 💰 **Know your pay before you move.** Every job shows the delivery fee upfront — accept it or skip it. No surprises.
- 🏦 **Your money, your bank.** Watch your earnings grow in your in-app wallet, then withdraw directly to any Nigerian bank account.
- 🕒 **Work when you want.** Go online anytime, take up to 3 deliveries at once, and log off when you're done. Bike, car, or van — you're welcome.
- ⭐ **Your reputation pays.** Customers choose riders by rating and delivery record — the better you deliver, the more jobs come to you.
- 📱 **One app does it all.** Job alerts, chat, calls, and live navigation — available in English and Hausa.

### How to start (3-step strip)
**1. SIGN UP** at entrepoints.ng (email or Google) → **2. VERIFY** your ID and vehicle → **3. GO ONLINE** and start earning.

### CTA block
> ## **Ready to ride?**
> ### Sign up today at **entrepoints.ng**
> *Free to join. Bring your bike, car, or van.*

### Design notes (for the flyer designer)
- **Hierarchy:** Headline dominant (top third) → subheadline → 5 bullets in the middle band → 3-step strip → CTA block anchored bottom with the URL in the largest weight after the headline.
- **Imagery:** one hero photo of a confident Nigerian rider on a bike with a branded delivery box, city street backdrop; secondary phone mockup showing the wallet screen ("Available balance" tile) if space allows.
- **Color:** use the app's sky-blue brand palette (#38bdf8 / #0ea5e9 accents on white) for recognition.
- **QR code:** place a QR code linking to `https://entrepoints.ng` beside the CTA — riders scan instead of typing.
- Keep to one page; bullets must survive at arm's length — minimum 11pt body.

## Claim guardrails (for anyone editing this copy)

Verified against the codebase — keep these boundaries when remixing the copy:

- ✅ "Keep 85% of every delivery fee" — the rider payout share is fixed in the platform at 85%.
- ✅ "Withdraw straight to your bank account" — Paystack-verified bank withdrawal exists.
- ❌ Never say "keep 100%" — the platform retains 15%.
- ❌ Never say "instant withdrawal" — payouts are approved before transfer; they are direct-to-bank but not instantaneous.
- ❌ Never print naira rates (per-km, minimum fare) — rates are admin-configurable and will go stale.
- ❌ Don't advertise rider-side dispute tools — none exist yet.
