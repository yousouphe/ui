# Aike Logistics — Rider Value Proposition

Recruitment messaging for two audiences: **individual riders** and **companies with fleets of riders** (partnership call). English. Rider signup CTA: **entrepoints.ng/aike/register**.

Every claim below is backed by a live feature or a stated business commitment. Do not mention the platform's percentage split anywhere — see "Claim guardrails" at the bottom.

---

## 1. Core value proposition

**Positioning statement (one line):**

> **Ride with Aike. Deliver on your terms, build your name, and withdraw your earnings daily — straight to your bank.**

**The four pillars:**

| Pillar | Message | Backed by |
|---|---|---|
| **EARN** | See the delivery fee **before you accept** any job — no surprises. Track your earnings today, this week, this month in your in-app wallet, and **withdraw daily** to any Nigerian bank account. | Fee shown upfront on every offer; wallet with ledger + Paystack-verified bank withdrawal; daily-withdrawal commitment |
| **FREEDOM** | You're the boss. Go online when you want, accept only the jobs you want, and run **up to 3 deliveries at once** when you're ready to hustle. Bike, car, or van — all welcome. | Accept/decline per job; online/offline toggle; 3-order cap; all vehicle types matched |
| **FAIRNESS** | Good work wins more work. Senders pick riders from a list ranked by **your rating and delivery record** — not just whoever is standing closest. Your five-star service is your best advert, and customers can still find you even when you're offline. | Merit-based match score (rating + delivery performance); offline riders remain visible |
| **TOOLS** | Everything in one app: instant job alerts, in-app chat with voice notes, in-app calls, live map navigation, and customer tracking — in English or Hausa. | Web push offers; chat + voice notes + read receipts; voice calls; live tracking; EN/HA UI |

**Fleet partnership (companies):**

> **Got riders? Let's put them to work.** Aike partners with logistics companies, courier services, and businesses running fleets of riders. Plug your riders into a steady stream of delivery jobs, with per-rider ratings, live tracking, and daily bank settlement — all managed from one platform. Partner with us at **entrepoints.ng**.

## 2. Flyer copy (print-ready)

### Headline
# **YOUR RIDE. YOUR TIME. YOUR MONEY.**

### Subheadline
**Become an Aike delivery rider — earn on every delivery and withdraw your money daily, straight to your bank account.**

### Body bullets
- 💰 **Know your pay before you move.** Every job shows the delivery fee upfront — accept it or skip it. No surprises.
- 🏦 **Withdraw daily.** Watch your earnings grow in your in-app wallet and cash out to any Nigerian bank account, every day.
- 🕒 **Work when you want.** Go online anytime, take up to 3 deliveries at once, and log off when you're done. Bike, car, or van — you're welcome.
- ⭐ **Your reputation pays.** Customers choose riders by rating and delivery record — the better you deliver, the more jobs come to you.
- 📱 **One app does it all.** Job alerts, chat, calls, and live navigation — available in English and Hausa.

### How to start (3-step strip)
**1. SIGN UP** at entrepoints.ng/aike/register (email or Google) → **2. VERIFY** your ID and vehicle → **3. GO ONLINE** and start earning.

### CTA block
> ## **Ready to ride?**
> ### Sign up today at **entrepoints.ng/aike/register**
> *Free to join. Bring your bike, car, or van.*

### Fleet partnership strip (bottom band of the flyer)
> 🚚 **OWN A FLEET?** Courier company? Business with riders on payroll? **Partner with Aike** and plug your whole fleet into a steady stream of delivery jobs — with live tracking, per-rider performance ratings, and daily bank settlement. **Reach us at entrepoints.ng.**

### Design notes (for the flyer designer)
- **Hierarchy:** Headline dominant (top third) → subheadline → 5 bullets in the middle band → 3-step strip → CTA block → fleet-partnership strip as a visually distinct bottom band (darker background, inverted colors) so both audiences see their message at a glance.
- **Imagery:** one hero photo of a confident Nigerian rider on a bike with a branded delivery box, city street backdrop; secondary phone mockup showing the wallet screen ("Available balance" tile) if space allows. For the fleet strip, a small motif of three riders in a row.
- **Color:** use the app's sky-blue brand palette (#38bdf8 / #0ea5e9 accents on white) for recognition.
- **QR code:** place a QR code linking to `https://entrepoints.ng/aike/register` beside the CTA — riders scan instead of typing the full address.
- Keep to one page; bullets must survive at arm's length — minimum 11pt body.

## Claim guardrails (for anyone editing this copy)

Keep these boundaries when remixing the copy:

- ✅ "See the fee before you accept" — every job offer shows the delivery fee upfront.
- ✅ "Withdraw daily, straight to your bank account" — Paystack-verified bank withdrawal exists; daily processing is a stated business commitment.
- ❌ **Never mention the platform's percentage split** (neither the rider share nor the platform fee) — the earnings message is the fee they see upfront plus daily bank withdrawal, nothing else.
- ❌ Never say "instant withdrawal" — payouts are processed daily to the bank, not instantly on tap.
- ❌ Never print naira rates (per-km, minimum fare) — rates are admin-configurable and will go stale.
- ❌ Don't advertise rider-side dispute tools — none exist yet.
- Fleet-partnership copy is a **call to contact**, not a feature list — don't promise fleet dashboards or bulk onboarding tools unless/until they're built.
