## 1. Information we collect
- **Account details:** name, email, phone number, and password (stored only as a secure hash).
- **Location:**
  - *Senders* — approximate/precise location while using the app to set pickup/drop-off and to see
    nearby riders.
  - *Riders* — precise location, including **in the background, only while you are online or on an
    active delivery**, so senders can track the trip and we can match nearby jobs. Location sharing
    stops when you go offline or the delivery ends.
- **Delivery details:** pickup/drop-off addresses, recipient name and phone, item details, and the
  in-app messages you exchange with the other party on a delivery.
- **Payments:** deliveries are paid through **Paystack**. We do **not** receive or store your full
  card details — only a payment reference and status. Riders provide a **bank account** for payouts,
  which is verified and used for withdrawals via Paystack.
- **Rider verification (KYC):** identity/vehicle information and document images riders submit for
  regulatory verification and admin review.
- **Device & technical data:** a push-notification token (to send delivery updates) and, if enabled,
  pseudonymous crash/diagnostic data.

## 2. How we use it
To create and secure your account; match senders with riders; price, route and track deliveries;
enable in-app chat and calls; process payments and payouts; verify riders; send delivery and account
notifications; provide support; prevent fraud and abuse; and meet legal obligations.

## 3. When we share it
- **With the other party on a delivery:** your name and phone number, and (for the rider) live
  location, are shown to the sender/rider you are matched with, so the delivery can happen.
- **Service providers (processors) acting for us:** Paystack (payments and payouts), Expo/Apple/Google
  (push-notification delivery), Mapbox (address search and routing — receives address queries and
  coordinates, not your identity), and, if enabled, Sentry (crash diagnostics).
- **Legal/safety:** where required by law or to protect users, the public, or our rights.
- We do **not** sell your data and do **not** use it for cross-app advertising or tracking.

## 4. Retention
We keep your information for as long as your account is active and as needed for the purposes above,
then delete or anonymise it, subject to legal/financial record-keeping requirements (e.g. payment
and KYC records).

## 5. Your rights
Subject to applicable law you may access, correct, or delete your data, object to or restrict certain
processing, and withdraw consent (for example, by turning off location sharing in your device
settings — note that riders cannot receive jobs without it). To request **account and data
deletion**, ⟨describe the in-app path or contact/URL⟩. We respond within the period required by law.

## 6. Security
We protect your data with encryption in transit (HTTPS), hashed passwords, access controls, and
restricted storage of verification documents. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect
your information.
